Minneapolis Sub Zero

Founded in Minneapolis in 1997, Sub Zero has emerged as the premier men's club ultimate team in the state of Minnesota. Throughout the years, we have competed on both national and international stages. We compete in the USA Ultimate Club Series in pursuit of a National Championship, traveling across the country to participate in regular season and postseason tournaments.

Every spring, our team hosts tryouts where hundreds of players compete to earn a roster spot.
